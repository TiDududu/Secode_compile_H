Primary functionality: The contract is a game called SpaceWar, where players can mine resources, upgrade their mining capabilities, and engage in player versus player combat.

1. SafeMath.add, Safely adds two numbers and checks for overflow.
2. SafeMath.sub, Safely subtracts two numbers and checks for underflow.
3. SafeMath.mul, Safely multiplies two numbers and checks for overflow.
4. NumericSequence.sumOfN, Calculates the sum of an arithmetic sequence.

The contract also includes various structs for MinerData, SpaceData, BoostData, PVPData, and TroopData, as well as mappings and arrays to store game data.

The constructor initializes the game parameters including space data and boost data, and sets the owner of the contract.
