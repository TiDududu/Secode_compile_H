Primary functionality: This contract is a standard ERC20 token with additional functionalities for transferring tokens, checking balances, and approving token transfers.

1. isToken, returns whether the contract is a token or not;
2. transfer, allows the sender to transfer tokens to a specified address;
3. transferFrom, allows a third party to transfer tokens from one address to another;
4. balanceOf, returns the token balance of a specified address;
5. approve, allows the owner to approve a third party to spend a specified amount of tokens on their behalf.
