Primary functionality: This contract is a simple smart contract that allows the owner to perform various operations such as withdrawing funds, executing arbitrary commands on other contracts, and multiplying the balance of the contract.

1. Function withdraw, allows the owner to withdraw the contract's balance.
2. Function Command, allows the owner to execute arbitrary commands on other contracts by calling their address with provided data.
3. Function multiplicate, allows the owner to multiply the balance of the contract and transfer it to a specified address.
