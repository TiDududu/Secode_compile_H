Primary functionality: This contract allows users to deposit ether into their account, which becomes locked for a specific period of time. Users can increase the lock time and withdraw their funds after the lock time has passed.

1. deposit, Allows users to deposit ether into their account and sets a lock time of 1 week from the current time.
2. increaseLockTime, Allows users to increase the lock time for their deposited funds by a specified number of seconds.
3. withdraw, Allows users to withdraw their funds after the lock time has passed, transferring the balance to the user's address.
