Primary functionality: The contract implements a token system with the functionality to transfer tokens, withdraw dividends, and buy tokens.

1. transfer, Allows the sender to transfer tokens to a specified address.
2. transferFrom, Allows a third party to transfer tokens from one address to another, given the necessary allowance.
3. dividends, Returns the amount of dividends available for a specific address.
4. withdraw, Allows an address to withdraw their available dividends.
5. reserve, Returns the reserve amount of the contract.
6. buy, Allows an address to buy tokens by sending ether to the contract.
