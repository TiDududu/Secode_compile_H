Primary functionality: This contract is an implementation of a standard ERC20 token with basic functionalities such as transferring tokens, checking balances, and approving token transfers.

1. SafeMath.mul, Safely multiply two numbers;
2. SafeMath.div, Safely divide two numbers;
3. SafeMath.sub, Safely subtract two numbers;
4. SafeMath.add, Safely add two numbers;
5. transfer, Transfer tokens to a specified address;
6. balanceOf, Get the balance of a specified address;
7. transferFrom, Transfer tokens from one address to another;
8. approve, Approve the passed address to spend the specified amount of tokens on behalf of the message sender.
