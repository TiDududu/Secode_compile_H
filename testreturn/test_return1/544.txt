Primary functionality: This Solidity contract is for a token called HNContract with the symbol "NN".

1. balanceOf, returns the balance of the specified address.
2. transfer, allows the sender to transfer tokens to a specified address.
3. transferFrom, allows a third party to transfer tokens from one address to another.
4. approve, allows the owner to approve a third party to spend a specified amount of tokens from the owner's address.
5. allowance, returns the remaining number of tokens that a third party is allowed to spend from the owner's address.
6. inflat, allows the contract creator to increase the total token supply.
7. burn, allows the token owner to burn a specified amount of tokens from their own address.

The contract also includes events for Transfer, Approval, Burn, and Inflat.
