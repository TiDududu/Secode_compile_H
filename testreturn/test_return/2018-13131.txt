Primary functionality: Transfer tokens from one address to another

1. transfer: Transfer tokens for a specified address, updating balances and emitting a Transfer event.
2. balanceOf: Get the balance of a specified address.
3. allowance: Get the amount of tokens allowed to be spent by a specified spender on behalf of an owner.
4. transferFrom: Transfer tokens from one address to another after allowance is checked and reducing the spender's allowance.
