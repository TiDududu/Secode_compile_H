Primary functionality: Transfer tokens from one account to another.
1. totalSupply, returns the total supply of tokens.
2. balanceOf, returns the balance of a specified address.
3. allowance, returns the amount of tokens that an owner allows a spender to spend.
4. transfer, transfers tokens from the caller's address to the specified address.
5. approve, allows a spender to withdraw from the caller's address multiple times, up to the specified amount.
6. transferFrom, transfers tokens from one address to another.
7. setAdmin, sets the admin status of a specified address.
8. pause, pauses the contract.
9. unpause, unpauses the contract.
10. _transfer, performs the internal transfer of tokens between two addresses.
