Primary functionality: Create and distribute QPay tokens.

1. mul, Safe math multiplication function;
2. div, Safe math division function;
3. sub, Safe math subtraction function;
4. add, Safe math addition function;
5. QPay, Constructor function to initialize the contract;
6. SetupQPY, Set up the QPY contract;
7. Transfer, Transfer tokens between addresses;
8. Approval, Approve the use of the token by another address;
9. Burn, Burn tokens;
10. onlyOwner, Modifier to restrict access to the owner of the contract.
