Primary functionality: Transfer ownership and manage tokens of Global Gold Cash.

1. transferOwnership, Allows the current owner to transfer ownership of the contract to a new address.
2. destruct, Allows the owner to destroy the contract and sends all remaining funds to the owner's address.
3. _transfer, Internal function to transfer tokens from one address to another.
4. transfer, Allows the sender to transfer tokens to a specified address.
5. transferFrom, Allows an approved address to transfer tokens from one address to another.
6. approve, Allows an address to approve another address to spend a specified amount of tokens from the sender's address.
7. approveAndCall, Allows an address to approve another address and call a function on the approved address.
8. burn, Allows the sender to burn a specified amount of their tokens.
9. burnFrom, Allows an approved address to burn a specified amount of tokens from a specified address.
